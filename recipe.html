Walnut & White Chocolate Brownies

Learn how to make non-traditional bronwies... Easy, simple, quick, and most importantly... delicious!!!

Ingredients:

1/4 cup of butter
1 cup of heavy cream
1 cup of sugar
1 tsp of vanilla
pinch of salt
1/2 cup of flour
2 eggs
1 cup of walnuts
1/2 cup of white chocolate, roughly chopped

How to prepare:

  1. Preheat the over to 350 F
  2. Toast the walnuts for 10-15 minutes.
  3. Grease a 9-inch squere baking dish with a little butter or oil and set aside
  4. Melt the butter ina pan over low heat and add cocoa powder, sugar,and cream. Stir until melted and smooth.
  5. Sift the flour into the pan and add eggs and vanilla.
  6. Gently stir until combined. Add toasted walnuts and white chocolate into the batter.
  7. Pour the batter into the baking dish and bake in the centre of our over for 40 mins.
  8. Leave brownies to cool completely, before slicing.
  9. These walnut and white chocolate brownies should keep well in an airtight container for about a week.
